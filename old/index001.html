HELPING TO PULL TOGETHER

A unique charity aiming to...

Put in place programmes to help develop lives for the benefit of social and health outcomes. To promote and encourage core values and skills for whole life benefits that can be used to improve health and education, combat obesity, and enhance personal development and self esteem.

Our mission statement

To provide opportunities and experiences of indoor and on the water coastal skiff rowing, to those that for reasons of youth, ability or circumstance would not otherwise have access to these activities.

To encourage the use of the sport of rowing to create a ‘pathway to change’ for disadvantaged 11-22 year olds to improve health, fitness, well-being and employability.

To foster a community sporting activity that is welcoming, supportive and accessible without regard to individual ability background or experience.
